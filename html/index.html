Our Story

Bistro à Table is a modern French restaurant headed by Chef Isadora Chai located in Petaling Jaya since 2011. Our multi award-winning establishment is intimate, inspired by the best global produce.

If you love what we do, then be sure to visit our sister restaurant Antara Restaurant.

Be sure to visit us on a regular basis and check out our latest, not to be missed Themed Degustation dinners on the first Monday of every month..
