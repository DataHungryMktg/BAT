Our Story

Established in 2011, Bistro à Table has been a forefront contender in Malaysia's food scene, offering creative and daring dishes to its patrons from our intimate establishment in Petaling Jaya. Headed by Chef Isadora Chai, our multi award-winning establishment is small and personal, affording us the creative and gastronomic luxury to change our ever-evolving menu.

If you love what we do, then be sure to visit our sister restaurant Antara.

Be sure to visit us on a regular basis and check out our latest, not to be missed themed Degustation dinners.

The Name

Bistro à Table (pronounced “ah t-ah-bl”) in French literally means "to the table!", a common term used in French households to gather family and friends around the table to feast. Initially founded as a bistro, the culinary offering evolved to serve the best of French dining. Unlike the stiff environment of the latter, we have chosen to have a casual but very tasteful dining environment. We firmly believe in concentrating our efforts into the plate being served.
